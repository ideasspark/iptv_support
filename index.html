Support - IPTV Player Pro - Smart TV

Welcome to the support page for IPTV Player Pro - Smart TV. Below are some guides to help you use the app more effectively.

1. How to Find an IPTV Playlist?

To find an IPTV playlist, you can follow these steps:

  1. Search Online: You can search for free or paid IPTV playlists on websites that provide IPTV services.
  2. Use Forums: IPTV forums often share free playlists. You can check forums like Reddit, Facebook Groups, or specialized IPTV forums.
  3. Purchase from Providers: If you want high-quality playlists, consider purchasing from reputable IPTV providers.
  4. Import the Playlist: Once you have a playlist, you can import it into the app using the "Import Playlist" option in the settings.

2. How to Add Xtream?

To add Xtream to the app, follow these steps:

  1. Open the IPTV Player Pro - Smart TV app.
  2. Go to "Settings."
  3. Select "Add Xtream Code" or "Add Xtream API."
  4. Enter your Xtream details:
    • Server URL: Your Xtream server address.
    • Username: Your Xtream username.
    • Password: Your Xtream password.
  5. Click "Save" to complete the process.

After adding Xtream, you can access channels and content from your Xtream account.
